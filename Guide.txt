There is no need to implement your own renderer for these projects, though you could attempt it if you 
wished - the focus should be on the generation of the assets, so you could implement these technques 
within an existing engine such as unity or Unreal and allow them to handle the rendering work for you.

* Procedural Cities & Buildings

Research approaches to procedurally generating city layouts that are filled with generated buildings,
undetake research into how cities evolve and the impact that has on the style and placement of 
buildings in your application.
